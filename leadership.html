    Our Philosophy Meet the Team

    Our Leadership Philosophy

    At Diet of the Word Church, we believe leadership is a sacred trust. Our leaders are called to serve, inspire, and equip others to fulfill God's purpose. We value integrity, humility, vision, and a heart for people. Every decision is prayerfully made, every action rooted in love, and every leader accountable to God and the church family.

    Servant Leadership

    We lead by serving, following Christ's example of humility and sacrificial love.

    Vision & Integrity

    We pursue God's vision with integrity, transparency, and unwavering faith.

    Empowering Others

    We equip and empower every member to discover and use their God-given gifts.

    Bishop Samuel Mwangi

    Lead Pastor & Visionary

    Bishop Samuel Mwangi has faithfully served as the Lead Pastor of Diet of the Word Church for over 15 years. With a passion for teaching God's Word and shepherding God's people, he is known for his wisdom, humility, and unwavering commitment to the spiritual growth of the church family. Bishop Samuel is a mentor to many, a visionary leader, and a servant at heart.

    "My greatest joy is seeing lives transformed by the power of God's Word. Our leadership team is here to serve, guide, and walk with you on your journey of faith. Welcome to Diet of the Word Church – a place to belong, grow, and make a difference."

    — Bishop Samuel Mwangi
