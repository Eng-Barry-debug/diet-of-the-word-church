    Welcome to Our Church Family

    We are a vibrant, Christ-centered community located in the heart of Nairobi, Kenya. Our church is built on the foundation of God's Word, and we are passionate about sharing the love of Christ with everyone who walks through our doors.

    Biblical Teaching

    We are committed to teaching God's Word faithfully and applying it to our daily lives.
