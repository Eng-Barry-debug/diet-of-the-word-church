Prayer Request Guidelines

What to Include

  • Specific details about your situation
  • Names of people involved (if appropriate)
  • Your desired outcome or prayer focus
  • Any specific scripture that encourages you

What to Avoid

  • Gossip or negative comments about others
  • Requests that violate biblical principles
  • Personal contact information beyond email
  • Requests for financial assistance
