Frequently Asked Questions

How soon will I get a response?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during our office hours. For urgent matters, please call us directly.

Is my information confidential?

Yes, all information shared with us is kept strictly confidential and used only for the purpose of your inquiry or request. We respect your privacy and never share your details without consent.

Can I visit the church in person?

Absolutely! We welcome visitors during office hours and for our Sunday worship service. Please check our Events page for special programs and guest days.

How do I request prayer?

You can use the contact form above and select "Prayer Request" as your reason, or visit our dedicated Prayer Requests page. Our prayer team prays for every request received.
