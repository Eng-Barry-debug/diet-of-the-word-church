Who We Are

Our Mission

To nourish lives with the Word of God, build a Christ-centered community, and serve our city and world with love.

Our Vision

To see lives transformed by the Gospel, families strengthened, and communities impacted for Christ in Nairobi and beyond.

Our Values

  • • Christ-centered Worship
  • • Biblical Teaching
  • • Authentic Community
  • • Compassionate Service
  • • Generosity & Integrity

Our Story

  1. Founded in 2010

    Diet of the Word International Church began as a small Bible study group in Nairobi, passionate about sharing the love and truth of Jesus Christ.

  2. Growth & Community Impact

    Over the years, we have grown into a vibrant, multi-generational church, launching ministries for youth, families, and outreach to our city.

  3. Looking Ahead

    We continue to pursue our calling to make disciples, serve our neighbors, and be a beacon of hope in Nairobi and beyond.

What We Believe

Our core beliefs shape everything we do as a church. These truths unite us and guide our mission, worship, and service.

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, authoritative Word of God—our foundation for faith and life.

God

We believe in one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Jesus Christ

We believe in the deity of Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, sinless life, miracles, atoning death, resurrection, and return.

Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone.

The Church

We believe in the spiritual unity of all believers in Christ.

Mission

We are called to love God, love people, and make disciples of all nations.
