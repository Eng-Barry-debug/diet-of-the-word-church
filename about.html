About Us

Learn more about our mission, vision, and the heart behind Diet of the Word Church.

Who We Are

Our Mission

To glorify God by making disciples of Jesus Christ through the faithful teaching of His Word and loving service to our community.

Our Vision

To be a Christ-centered church where people are nourished by the Word, empowered by the Spirit, and equipped to impact the world for God's glory.

Our Values

Faithfulness, love, integrity, service, and a passion for God's Word guide everything we do as a church family.

Our Story

A Journey of Faith

Founded in 2010, Diet of the Word Church began as a small gathering of believers committed to the faithful teaching of God's Word. What started as a humble Bible study group has grown into a vibrant community of over 500 members, serving the Nairobi area with love, compassion, and the truth of the Gospel.

Over the past 15 years, we've witnessed God's faithfulness as He has transformed lives, built families, and established our church as a beacon of hope in our community. Our journey has been marked by prayer, perseverance, and a deep commitment to biblical truth.

What We Believe

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and our final authority for faith and practice.

Salvation

We believe salvation comes through faith in Jesus Christ alone, who died for our sins and rose again.

The Church

We believe the church is the body of Christ, called to worship, disciple, and serve together.

Love & Service

We believe in demonstrating God's love through compassionate service to our community and world.

Discipleship

We believe in making disciples who grow in faith and multiply the ministry of Christ.

Eternity

We believe in the return of Christ and the promise of eternal life for all who believe.
