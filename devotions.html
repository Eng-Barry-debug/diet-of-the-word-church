Faith Over Fear

By Bishop David Mwangi â€¢ June 2, 2024

"For God has not given us a spirit of fear, but of power and of love and of a sound mind." - 2 Timothy 1:7

Fear is one of the most powerful emotions that can paralyze us and prevent us from living the abundant life God has planned for us. But God has not given us a spirit of fear. Instead, He has given us power, love, and a sound mind. Walk in the confidence that comes from knowing you are a child of God.
