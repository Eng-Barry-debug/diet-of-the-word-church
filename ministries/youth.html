Youth Ministry

Inspiring and equipping the next generation to live boldly for Christ and impact their world.

Welcome to Youth Ministry

Our Youth Ministry is designed to engage young people ages 13-25 in a dynamic, faith-filled community where they can grow spiritually, build lasting friendships, and discover their God-given purpose. We believe in the power of young people to change the world for Christ.

Dynamic Worship

Contemporary worship experiences that connect young hearts to God through music, prayer, and authentic expression of faith.

Authentic Community

Build genuine friendships with peers who share your faith and understand the unique challenges of being a young Christian today.

Leadership Development

Discover and develop your God-given gifts and talents through leadership opportunities, mentoring, and hands-on ministry experience.
