Welcome to Teenagers Ministry

Our Teenagers Ministry is specifically designed for teens ages 13-17, providing a safe, fun, and faith-filled environment where they can grow spiritually, build authentic friendships, and discover their identity in Christ. We understand the unique challenges teens face and create programs that speak directly to their hearts and minds.

Relevant Bible Study

Interactive Bible studies that address real teen issues and help them apply God's Word to their daily lives.

Safe Community

A welcoming environment where teens can be themselves, make friends, and feel accepted for who they are.

Fun & Engaging

Exciting activities, games, and events that make learning about faith enjoyable and memorable.
