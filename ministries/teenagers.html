Welcome to Teenagers Ministry

Our Teenagers Ministry (ages 13-17) is a safe, fun, and faith-filled space for teens to grow spiritually, build authentic friendships, and discover their identity in Christ. We address real teen issues and create programs that speak directly to their hearts and minds.

  • 📖 Weekly Bible Studies & Discussions
  • 🎤 Music, Worship, and Creative Arts
  • 🤝 Service Projects & Community Impact
  • 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Mentoring, Life Skills, and Leadership
  • 🏕️ Camps, Retreats, and Social Events

Why Join?

  • ✔ Make real friends
  • ✔ Grow in faith and confidence
  • ✔ Discover your gifts
  • ✔ Have fun and make a difference
