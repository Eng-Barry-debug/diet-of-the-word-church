Welcome to Sunday School

Our Sunday School ministry is dedicated to nurturing children (ages 3-12) in a safe, loving, and faith-filled environment. We partner with parents to lay a strong spiritual foundation, teaching biblical truths through creative lessons, worship, and hands-on activities. Every child is valued, known, and encouraged to grow in their relationship with Jesus.

Creative Bible Lessons

Children learn foundational Bible stories and truths in a creative, age-appropriate environment.

Worship & Music

Kids enjoy lively worship, singing, and music that helps them express their love for God.

Fun Activities

Games, crafts, and hands-on activities make learning about God fun and memorable.
