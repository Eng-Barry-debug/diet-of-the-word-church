Welcome to Toddlers Ministry

Our Toddlers Ministry (ages 1-3) is a nurturing, joy-filled space where little ones are cared for by loving volunteers. We introduce toddlers to Jesus through play, music, stories, and gentle guidance, helping them feel safe, valued, and loved as part of God's family.

Loving Care

Trained volunteers provide attentive, gentle care so every child feels safe and cherished.

Play & Learn

Songs, stories, and play introduce toddlers to God's love in a fun, age-appropriate way.

Safe Environment

Clean, secure spaces designed for little ones to explore, play, and learn safely.
